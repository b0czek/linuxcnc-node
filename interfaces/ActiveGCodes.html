    Interface ActiveGCodes

    Active G-codes for each modal group in the LinuxCNC interpreter. Each property represents the currently active G-code from its respective modal group. Integer values reflect the nominal G-code numbers multiplied by 10. (Examples: 10 = G1, 430 = G43, 923 = G92.3)
